Facts on Dog Breeds

  • Basenji – The only dog that doesn’t bark, but yodels!
  • Dalmatian Puppies – Born completely white; spots develop later.
  • Chow Chow – Has a rare blue-black tongue, like the Shar Pei.
  • Newfoundland – Natural lifeguards with webbed feet for swimming.
  • Afghan Hound – Their long, silky fur keeps them warm in cold regions.
  • Greyhound – Can outrun a cheetah over long distances!
  • Labrador Retriever – Has an "otter tail" for swimming.
  • Corgis – Once used as Viking war dogs!
  • Boxers – Got their name because they "box" with their front paws.
  • Great Dane – Originally bred for hunting wild boars.
  • Huskies – Can survive in freezing temperatures as low as -75°F.
  • Dachshund – Originally bred to hunt badgers.
  • Shiba Inu – Famous for their loud "Shiba scream" when excited.
  • Poodle – Originally bred as water retrievers, not just show dogs!
  • Border Collie – The smartest breed; some can learn 1,000+ words.
